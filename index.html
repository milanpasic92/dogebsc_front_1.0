Why DogeBSC?

Doge is the people's coin, so it should be treated as such. A DeFi gateway for Dogecoin holders, and a low fee alternative to Hoge holders. As a bonus, you help us raise money for actual dogs all over the world. Pretty sick deal if you ask me :)

What is DogeBSC?

Hmm... well it's kind of like Dogecoin, but on the Binance Smart Chain. Pretty simple really.

How to buy

DogeBSC is on the Binance Smart Chain, to purchase this and to pay gas fees for transfers you will need BNB. You can obtain BNB through Binance, following the next steps:

Get some BNB

Purchase BNB on Binance, KuCoin or the exchange of your choice.

I have some BNB, now what?

  • Transfer your BNB to your MetaMask wallet.
  • Open Pancakeswap & accept the pop-up dialog to view the swapping interface (Wait a few seconds.) Otherwise click on connect on the top right.
  • Click the Gear icon in the top right of the exchange interface and change the slippage to at least 2%.
  • Enter the amount of BNB you wish to buy with, click the Swap button, and then approve the transaction in MetaMask.
